LuffaLense Web App

LuffaLense is an easy-to-use mobile application designed to identify possible leaf diseases in both Smooth Luffa and Sponge Luffa plants through image analysis. The app currently supports the following disease scopes:
For Smooth Luffa — Alternaria, Angular Spot, Holed, Mosaic Virus, and Fresh (healthy) leaves. For Sponge Luffa — Insect Damage, Mosaic Disease, Downy Mildew, Fresh (healthy) leaves, and Bacterial Leaf Spot. To use the app, simply upload a clear image of the affected luffa leaf. LuffaLense will analyze the image and provide the most likely disease category, helping users recognize and respond to crop issues quickly and accurately.
